BIO

Kate Henshaw, born on July 19 1971 is a veteran Nigerian actress. One of Nollywood's longest-serving personalities, Kate in 2008 won the Africa Movie Academy Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role. In 2013, she launched her book, The Life Of An Actress: My Story, at the Muson Center, Onikan, Lagos.

The actress' first appearance in Nollywood can be traced to her auditioning for the lead role in a movie in the year 1993. She got the role, and featured in the movie, When The Sun Sets. She soon rose to stardom and fame after participating in scores of movies, among which are New Horizons, The Meeting, Broken Tears (1 &2), River of Tear, Dorothy, A Brighter Sun (1&2), One Life (1 &2), Show Me Heaven (1&2), You Broke My Heart (1&2), Ghetto Love, Domitilla, Silent Night (1&2), I Need My Husband, etc.
